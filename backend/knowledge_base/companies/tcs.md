# TCS — Placement Guide for LPU Students

## Overview
TCS is the **largest recruiter at LPU**, hiring 500-800+ students every year across all branches. It offers multiple tracks with different CTC packages.

## Roles & CTC

| Role | Track | CTC |
|---|---|---|
| System Engineer | Ninja | 3.36 LPA |
| Digital Specialist Engineer | Digital | 7 LPA |
| Prime | Prime | 9.5 LPA |
| Ignite (for 2+ years exp) | — | varies |

## Eligibility
- 60%+ aggregate in 10th, 12th, and graduation (no rounding)
- No active backlogs at time of application
- All branches eligible
- Gap years allowed with valid reason

## Selection Process

### TCS NQT (National Qualifier Test)
- **Duration**: 180 minutes total
- **Sections**:
  - Numerical Ability: 26 questions / 40 min
  - Verbal Ability: 24 questions / 30 min
  - Reasoning Ability: 30 questions / 50 min
  - Coding: 1-2 problems / 60 min (Ninja requires 1, Digital requires advanced)
- **Platform**: TCS iON

### Ninja Track
- NQT score + basic coding (easy level)
- 1 coding problem (arrays/strings/basic DP)
- Technical Interview (30-40 min): OS, DBMS, OOP basics, any one language

### Digital Track
- NQT Advanced Coding: **2 DSA problems in 60 min** (medium difficulty)
- Topics: Graph BFS/DFS, DP, Trees, Strings
- Technical Interview: DSA discussion + projects + SQL
- Managerial Round

### Prime Track
- Data Science / ML / Advanced AI background needed
- NLP, ML model knowledge expected
- Python + TensorFlow/PyTorch experience preferred

## Preparation Tips

### Aptitude (NQT)
- **Numerical**: Percentages, Profit-Loss, Time-Speed-Distance, Probability
- **Verbal**: Fill in blanks, sentence correction, reading comprehension
- **Reasoning**: Number series, syllogisms, coding-decoding, arrangements
- TCS has specific patterns — practice on **PrepInsta TCS NQT** section
- IndiaBix for general practice

### Coding
- Ninja: Focus on arrays, strings, recursion, basic DP (easy LeetCode)
- Digital: Medium LeetCode — Graph traversals, DP problems, Tree problems

### Resources
- TCS iON free mock tests
- PrepInsta TCS NQT previous papers (paid but worth it)
- GeeksforGeeks TCS section
- NeetCode for coding patterns

## Interview Tips
- Be confident in OOP concepts (class, object, inheritance, polymorphism)
- Know at least one language well (Java/Python/C++)
- Prepare 2-3 project descriptions with your role and technologies used
- SQL basics: SELECT, JOIN, GROUP BY are commonly asked
- Be honest if you don't know something — interviewers appreciate honesty

## Timeline at LPU
- TCS typically visits **August-October** for bulk hiring
- Watch myplacements.lpu.in announcements closely
- Register early on TCS NextStep portal (nextstep.tcs.com)
